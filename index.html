ZombAttack

ZombAttack is a fun, zombie apocalyptic game, featuring eight different weapons, including a chainsaw and a minigun. ZombAttack also features two different maps, including the City and the Farm. ZombAttack encourages players to work together to defeat the zombies that attack you, but also prevents internet trolls, as the players can't hurt each other.
